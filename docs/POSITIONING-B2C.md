# BoxBlueBook: B2C Positioning

## The Pitch

> **"Never overpay for cigars again."**
> 
> BoxBlueBook compares prices across every major online retailer so you always find the best deal on your favorite smokes.

---

## Target User

**The Savvy Cigar Enthusiast**
- Smokes 2-10 cigars/week
- Buys online (boxes, samplers, singles)
- Price-conscious but quality-focused
- Already shops Famous, CI, JR, etc.
- Frustrated by price hunting across tabs

**Not targeting:**
- Casual smokers (too infrequent)
- Ultra-premium collectors (price insensitive)
- B2B/retailers (different product)

---

## Core Value Props

### 1. **Price Comparison**
> "See prices from 6 retailers in one place"

- Famous Smoke, CI, JR Cigars, Holts, Atlantic, CigarPage
- Single stick AND box pricing
- Real-time stock status
- Sale/coupon detection

### 2. **Price Alerts**
> "Get notified when your cigars go on sale"

- Set target price → email when it drops
- Back-in-stock alerts for allocated cigars
- Weekly "deals" digest email

### 3. **Price History**
> "Is this actually a good deal?"

- 90-day price charts per retailer
- See if "sale" price is actually lower than usual
- Track price trends (up/down/stable)

### 4. **The Encyclopedia** (differentiator)
> "Learn about what you're smoking"

- Production info, box codes, aging potential
- Tasting notes, pairings
- Community reviews
- This is what makes it more than just a price tool

---

## User Journey

```
1. DISCOVER
   Google: "padron 1926 best price"
   → Lands on BoxBlueBook product page
   → Sees all retailer prices

2. COMPARE
   → Clicks through to best-priced retailer
   → (We earn affiliate commission)

3. RETURN
   → Creates account for price alerts
   → Builds watchlist
   → Gets weekly deals email

4. ENGAGE
   → Explores encyclopedia content
   → Reads reviews, leaves reviews
   → Shares finds with friends

5. SUBSCRIBE (optional)
   → Pro tier for power users
   → Unlimited alerts, API access
```

---

## Monetization

### Primary: Affiliate Revenue
- Famous, CI, JR, etc. all have affiliate programs
- 5-10% commission on referred sales
- User clicks "Buy at Famous" → we earn

### Secondary: Freemium Subscriptions

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 10 price checks/day, 3 alerts |
| **Pro** | $4.99/mo | Unlimited checks, unlimited alerts, price history, no ads |
| **Connoisseur** | $9.99/mo | Pro + early sale notifications, exclusive deals, API |

### Tertiary: Display Ads
- Tasteful cigar-related ads
- Only on free tier
- Removed for Pro+

## SEO Strategy

### Target Keywords (transactional)
- "[cigar name] price"
- "[cigar name] best deal"
- "cheapest place to buy [cigar]"
- "[cigar name] on sale"
- "[retailer] vs [retailer] prices"

### Target Keywords (informational)
- "[cigar name] review"
- "[cigar name] tasting notes"
- "best cigars under $10"
- "[cigar brand] box codes"

### Content Strategy
- Product pages rank for "[cigar] price"
- Blog posts rank for informational queries
- Comparison pages: "Famous vs CI: Who's Cheaper?"

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | Our Advantage |
|------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Famous/CI/JR** | Sell cigars | We're neutral, show ALL prices |
| **Cigar Scanner** | Price lookup app | We have history + alerts + content |
| **CigarBid** | Auctions | We cover retail, they cover secondary |
| **Reddit r/cigars** | Community deals | We're automated, always current |

**Key differentiator:** Nobody else combines price comparison + historical data + cigar encyclopedia in one place.

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Knowledgeable friend, not salesy retailer

- ✅ "Here's what we found across 6 stores"
- ❌ "BUY NOW! LOWEST PRICES!"

- ✅ "Looks like Famous has the best deal today"
- ❌ "Click here for EXCLUSIVE SAVINGS"

- ✅ "This cigar typically sells for $12-15"
- ❌ "INCREDIBLE VALUE!!!"

**Personality:**
- Helpful
- Trustworthy (we don't sell cigars, we help you find deals)
- Knowledgeable
- Slightly nerdy about cigars
